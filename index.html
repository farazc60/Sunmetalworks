SHEET METAL FABRICATION

Sun Metal Works has been fabricating products from sheet metal for over 25 years. This includes
aluminium sheet metal, which is popular due to its versatility and excellent resistance to
corrosion. Aluminium is strong, light and commonly used in light fabrication projects.

At Sun Metal Works, we offer fast turnround, excellent service and precisely engineered products.
